GerberaGlow Natural Face Tint

Product Overview

Discover the allure of nature's palette with our GerberaGlow Natural Face Tint. This unique product is your passport to attaining an effervescent, youthful complexion reminiscent of the freshness of dawn.

Inspiration Behind the Tint:

Drawing from the natural splendor of Gerbera daisies, known for their brilliant and striking colors, our face tint captures the essence of these blooms. Just as a Gerbera daisy adds a touch of life and color to any bouquet, our tint is designed to seamlessly amplify your skin's natural charm.

Key Features

  • Natural Glow: Infused with gerbera extract, known for its skin-brightening properties, our face tint brings a soft, luminous finish to your skin.
  • Lightweight & Buildable: Our easily blendable formula allows you to achieve the desired coverage, from sheer to medium, without ever feeling heavy or greasy.
  • Skin-Loving Ingredients: Alongside gerbera extract, we've blended a nourishing mix of botanical oils, aloe vera, and hyaluronic acid to hydrate and condition your skin.
  • Long-Lasting: Designed for all-day wear, GerberaGlow ensures that you maintain a fresh, dewy look from morning till night.
  • Eco-Friendly: Packaged in recyclable glass jars and made with sustainably sourced ingredients, we aim to be as kind to the planet as we are to your skin.

Ingredients

  • Organic Gerbera Extract
  • Aloe Vera
  • Jojoba Oil
  • Hyaluronic Acid
  • Rosewater
  • Mineral Pigments
  • Vitamin E

Good to Know

  • Free from parabens, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances
