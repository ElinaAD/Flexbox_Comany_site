About Us

At Tree of Life Cosmetic Company, our story is rooted in a deep reverence for nature and its myriad wonders. It was during a sojourn through the dense rainforests of Brazil that our founder, Julie Gustavson, became captivated by the time-honored beauty practices of the indigenous tribes. These communities, untouched by modernity, had mastered the art of sourcing the most pristine ingredients from the forests around them — from towering trees to medicinal herbs — discovering nature’s blueprint for timeless beauty. Julie was set on a mission: to introduce these invaluable secrets to the world, but in a contemporary and approachable manner. Merging traditional wisdom with avant-garde research, Tree of Life Cosmetic Company came into being in 2010. Now, we are a living embodiment of Julie's dream of pure, natural beauty. Our dedication is unwavering, with each product subjected to rigorous scrutiny. We pledge that every product we offer contains at least 80% organic ingredients, reaffirming our commitment to bringing you closer to nature's genuine embrace, and reshaping beauty routines with the authentic spirit of nature.
